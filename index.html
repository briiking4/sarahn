Following my passion for the study of communication theory, I have completed a B.A. in Communications from USC Annenberg School of Communication and Journalism. I have centered my concentration on strategy, marketing and rhetoric; cultivating the ability to decode the concepts in communication theory and apply them to these areas. A college highlight was studying abroad in London, England, where I learned about social change and campaigns, branding and global strategy directly from industry leaders.
